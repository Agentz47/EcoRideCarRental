Class Diagram for EcoRide Car Rental System

Classes:
- EcoRide_Vehicle
  - Attributes: carId (String), model (String), category (String), dailyRentalPrice (double), availabilityStatus (String)
  - Methods: getCarId(), setCarId(), getModel(), setModel(), getCategory(), setCategory(), getDailyRentalPrice(), setDailyRentalPrice(), getAvailabilityStatus(), setAvailabilityStatus(), isAvailable(), toString()

- EcoRide_Customer
  - Attributes: nicOrPassport (String), name (String), contactNumber (String), email (String)
  - Methods: getNicOrPassport(), setNicOrPassport(), getName(), setName(), getContactNumber(), setContactNumber(), getEmail(), setEmail(), toString()

- EcoRide_Booking
  - Attributes: bookingId (String), customer (EcoRide_Customer), vehicle (EcoRide_Vehicle), startDate (LocalDate), endDate (LocalDate), totalKm (int), deposit (double)
  - Methods: getBookingId(), setBookingId(), getCustomer(), setCustomer(), getVehicle(), setVehicle(), getStartDate(), setStartDate(), getEndDate(), setEndDate(), getTotalKm(), setTotalKm(), getDeposit(), getNumberOfDays(), isValidBooking(), toString()

- EcoRide_Invoice
  - Attributes: booking (EcoRide_Booking), basePrice (double), extraKmCharge (double), discount (double), tax (double), totalAmount (double)
  - Methods: getBooking(), getBasePrice(), getExtraKmCharge(), getDiscount(), getTax(), getTotalAmount(), toString()

- EcoRide_RentalSystem
  - Attributes: vehicles (ArrayList<EcoRide_Vehicle>), vehicleMap (HashMap<String, EcoRide_Vehicle>), customers (ArrayList<EcoRide_Customer>), customerMap (HashMap<String, EcoRide_Customer>), bookings (ArrayList<EcoRide_Booking>), bookingMap (HashMap<String, EcoRide_Booking>)
  - Methods: addVehicle(), getVehicle(), updateVehicle(), removeVehicle(), getAllVehicles(), registerCustomer(), getCustomer(), makeBooking(), getBooking(), searchBookingsByName(), generateInvoice()

Associations:
- EcoRide_Booking has-a EcoRide_Customer (1:1)
- EcoRide_Booking has-a EcoRide_Vehicle (1:1)
- EcoRide_Invoice has-a EcoRide_Booking (1:1)
- EcoRide_RentalSystem has-many EcoRide_Vehicle (1:*)
- EcoRide_RentalSystem has-many EcoRide_Customer (1:*)
- EcoRide_RentalSystem has-many EcoRide_Booking (1:*)

Design Rationale:
- Encapsulation: All classes use private fields with public getters/setters.
- Inheritance: Not used here, but could be extended for different vehicle types.
- Polymorphism: Methods like toString() are overridden for custom output.
- Data Structures: ArrayList for ordered lists, HashMap for O(1) lookups by ID.
- Algorithms: Fee calculation in Invoice uses conditional logic for discounts and tax.
